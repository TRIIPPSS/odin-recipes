Overnight Oats

A simple no cook alternative to typical oatmeal!

This overnight oats recipe is a great healthy breakfast that you can prepare in advance and it can last all week.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk
  • Chia seeds
  • Greek or vegan yogurt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Sweetener
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Combine old fashioned oats and chia seeds
  2. Add the greek or vegan yogurt and vanilla extract
  3. Add the sweetener of choice and the milk
  4. Mix the overnight oats together until you see no clumps
  5. Stick in the fridge overnight, add your toppings and enjoy!
